A straightforward way to start pulling things apart: ask questions and *assign names* to the following *aspects* of a program: who, what, when, where, why, and how.

Once named, aspects become visible. "The pricing logic" can be discussed, moved, and changed. Unnamed aspects stay hidden inside other code, silently coupling things that should be separate.
