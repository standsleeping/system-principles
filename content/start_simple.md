Per Gall, we must always start simple, and be attentive to changes that add _useful complexity_ to our model of the world over time.

Size our iterations such that the "full principle cycle" below results in a user trying out the new version,
and noticing a small-enough yet useful-enough difference from the previous version as to quickly test and approve.
